How to Use Our Products

Complete guide to using our wellness products safely and effectively

1

Browse & Order

Visit our products page, select items you want, and add them to your cart. Review your order before checkout.

2

Checkout Process

Go to cart, choose payment method (Cash on Delivery or EFT), and provide delivery address including apartment details.

3

Receive Delivery

Wait for delivery. For Cash on Delivery, pay when you receive. For EFT, ensure payment is made before delivery.

4

Use Products

Follow product-specific instructions below for optimal results and safety.

Product Usage Guidelines

Green Leaves

  • Consume as directed on packaging
  • Store in cool, dry place
  • Keep away from direct sunlight
  • Use within 30 days of opening

Purple Water Substance

  • Shake well before use
  • Consume recommended amount daily
  • Refrigerate after opening
  • Use within 14 days of opening

White Vitamin Pills

  • Take one pill daily with food
  • Do not exceed recommended dosage
  • Store in original container
  • Keep away from children

Sugar Sachets

  • Add to coffee or tea as desired
  • Store in dry place
  • Each sachet for single use
  • Keep away from moisture

Important Safety Information

Consult Professional

Consult healthcare professional before starting any new wellness regimen, especially if you have medical conditions.

Keep Away from Children

All products should be stored out of reach of children. These are adult wellness products.

Allergy Warning

Check ingredients if you have allergies. Discontinue use if any adverse reactions occur.

Storage Instructions

Store all products according to package instructions. Do not use expired products.
